PID =  How do I reduce the error between where I am and where I want to be ?

Error= target - current 
Then PID controls how to correct it .
P - Proportional 
  - Reacts to the current error 
  - Bigger error - bigger correction 
  - Simple but this causes oscillation 

I - Integral 
  - Reacts to past error 
  - fixes steady state error 
  -Accumulated the error over time 

D - Derivative 
  - aaareacts to future trend (rate of change)
  - Prevents overshooting and stability
