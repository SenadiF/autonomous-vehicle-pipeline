Reinforcement learning - Learning from consequences 

Agent -> Takes action -> gets reward -> Updated behavior

Over time :
   Tries new things(Exploration)
   Uses what works(Exploitation)

RL has 2 main categories :

1. Value Based Methods 
  - They learn how good is this state or action ?
      - Q learning , SARSA , Deep Q network 
2. Policy -based method 
  - They learn , What action should i take directly ?
   - eg : Policy gradients , Reinforce 

Actor - critic (Hybrid)
  Actor - Decides action 
  Critic - Evaluates action 

GOAL is the same but only differ by the way they learn .
